# Seasons by B — Instagram Marketing Strategy

**Account:** @seasonsbyb · **Site:** seasonsbyb.co.uk
**Positioning:** London's finest beauty, personally sourced and delivered to your door in Lebanon.

---

## 1. The One-Line Strategy

Make @seasonsbyb the account a Lebanese woman checks **before** she asks a cousin abroad to bring her makeup. Every post answers one of three thoughts: *"I want that"*, *"Can I actually get that here?"*, or *"Which one should I get?"*

## 2. Audience

| Segment | Who | What they respond to |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | Women 22–45 in Lebanon (Beirut, Jounieh, Tripoli), beauty-engaged, follow CT/Huda/Rare Beauty | Product spotlights with **clear USD prices**, delivery proof, shade content |
| Secondary | Lebanese diaspora gifting family back home | "Send a gift home" angles, gift sets, occasions |
| Tertiary | Men buying fragrance/gifts | Fragrance posts (Bleu de Chanel, Creed), "DM us, we handle it" |

**Core objection to defeat in every piece of content:** *"Can I trust this / will it actually arrive?"* → answer with tracking proof, unboxings, customer reposts, and the 10–14 day promise stated plainly.

## 3. Content Pillars

| Pillar | % | What it is | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Most Wanted** | 30% | Bestsellers from the live catalogue (the site now ranks these — use the "Most wanted" strip per category as your editorial calendar) | Single-product posts, carousels, spotlight stories |
| **Proof & Process** | 20% | Selfridges hauls, packing orders, tracking screenshots, delivery-day reposts | Stories (daily), Reels |
| **Help Me Choose** | 20% | Shade Finder promos, This-or-That polls, "which is your shade?", dupes vs originals, Béa the AI consultant | Stories with stickers, Reels, carousel guides |
| **New In / Back in Stock** | 20% | New arrivals from the daily scrape, restocks of cult items | Posts + countdown stories |
| **Community & Occasions** | 10% | Mother's Day, Eid, Christmas, weddings season, customer features, giveaways | All formats |

## 4. Cadence (sustainable, ~5 hrs/week)

| Day | Feed | Stories (2–4 frames/day) |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Most Wanted product post | Poll: this-or-that |
| Tue | — | Behind-the-scenes: orders being placed in London |
| Wed | Reel (see §6) | Shade Finder push + "DM 🐝" |
| Thu | New In post | New-in countdown sticker |
| Fri | Carousel (guide/top-5) | Weekend treat + price drop |
| Sat | — | Customer repost / unboxing |
| Sun | Icon spotlight (La Mer, Baccarat…) | Quiet: question sticker "what should we source next?" |

**Rules:**
- Every product post shows the **price in USD** and ends with the same CTA: **"DM 🐝 to order"** (the bee is the brand shorthand — train followers that 🐝 in DMs = an order).
- Reply to every DM within the hour during 6–10pm Beirut time (peak).
- Stories ALWAYS use a sticker (poll/quiz/question/countdown) — stickers drive the algorithm and DMs.

## 6. Reels Playbook (highest reach — aim for 1/week minimum)

You don't need to appear on camera. Phone + products + CapCut:

1. **The Haul** (after each London buying run): fast cuts of bags/products, text overlay "Everything Lebanon ordered this week 🇱🇧" — ends "DM 🐝 to get in next week's order".
2. **Pack an order with me**: ASMR-style packing one customer's order, name hidden. Most trusted format for "will it arrive?" doubt.
3. **$50 / $100 / $500 at Selfridges**: three products at three budgets, one category.
4. **Shade Finder demo**: 30-sec screen recording finding a shade match, captioned "wrong shade era = over".
5. **This came back in stock**: 7-sec single-product hype clip (use for Baccarat/CT restocks).

Hooks (first second, text overlay): "POV: you live in Lebanon and want REAL La Mer" · "Stop asking cousins to fill their suitcase" · "What $100 gets you at Selfridges right now".

## 8. Profile Setup (do once)

- **Bio:** "London's finest beauty, delivered to Lebanon 🇱🇧 · Selfridges & more · 10–14 days, tracked · DM 🐝 to order" + link to seasonsbyb.co.uk
- **Highlights:** How It Works · Most Wanted · Reviews/Deliveries · Shade Finder · New In
- Pin 3 posts: post-3 (how it works), post-1 (hero product), best customer-proof post once you have one.

## 9. Engagement Routine (20 min/day)

1. Answer all DMs + comments (price questions = sales — never leave one hanging).
2. Comment on 5 posts from Lebanese beauty creators/MUAs (future collab pipeline).
3. Repost every customer story mention to Highlights within 24h.
4. Weekly: DM 2–3 Beirut MUAs/micro-influencers (5–30k) — offer a product in exchange for an honest story. MUAs are your highest-trust channel.

## 10. Metrics (check weekly, 10 min)

| Metric | Target (90 days) |
|---|---|
| DMs containing 🐝 / "price" | The #1 number — track weekly, everything serves this |
| Story link taps → site | 30+/week |
| Follower growth | +8–10%/month |
| Saves on product posts | Saves > likes ratio rising (saves = purchase intent) |

**Optimization rule:** every 2 weeks, double down on the pillar with the most DMs generated, not the most likes.
